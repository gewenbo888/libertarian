# On Liberty · 论自由 — A Libertarian Manifesto

A comprehensive single-page manifesto covering libertarian thought end-to-end: first principles, the canon, the thinkers, the schools, Austrian economics, crypto-libertarianism, policy, great quotations, further reading, and honest answers to the great objections.

Designed in the tradition of a 19th-century broadside meeting a cypherpunk mailing list — Bodoni display serifs over parchment, oxblood and gold accents, a dark monospace section for the crypto frontier.

## Structure

1. **Creed** — opening hero with Locke + a double-column bilingual creed
2. **First Principles** — 8 axioms (self-ownership, NAP, homesteading, exchange, spontaneous order, rule of law, exit, epistemic humility)
3. **The Canon** — 12 foundational books, 1689–2008
4. **The Thinkers** — 16 figures across four centuries
5. **Schools** — spectrum from classical liberalism to crypto-anarchy
6. **The Austrian Vision** — the knowledge & calculation problems
7. **Crypto-Libertarianism** — Bitcoin, DAOs, network states, privacy tech
8. **Policy Map** — 12 topics with state view vs. libertarian position
9. **Great Quotations** — Bastiat, Rand, Friedman, Jefferson, Brandeis
10. **Further Reading** — institutes, podcasts, contemporary books
11. **Objections & Answers** — 7 honest responses to the common critiques
